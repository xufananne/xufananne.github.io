About Me

I am currently pursuing a Master's degree in International Chinese Language Education at Beijing Normal University, specializing in Second Language Acquisition & Cognitive Neuroscience under the supervision of Professor Feng Liping.

My research interests focus on cognitive mechanisms of language predictive processing, effects of multimodal information on L2 reading, and embodied cognition in bilingual processing. I employ experimental methods such as eye-tracking and EEG to explore cognitive mechanisms of language processing.
